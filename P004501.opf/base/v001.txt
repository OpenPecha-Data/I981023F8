GTSANG-SMYON was born in Gtsang at Nyang-stod Mkhar-
kha of the lineage of NYANG, a lineage that had produced
such famous Tibetan teachers as Nyang-ral Nyi-ma-'od-zer
(1124-1192). His father's name was Sngags-'chang Sangs-
rgyas-dpal-ldan and his mother was Sangs-rgyas-'dren. He
was one of three brothers, all destined to become famous
religious personages; his younger brother, Dkon-mchog-
rgyal-mtshan, was born in 1453. His elder brother was Sog-
po-rgyal-mtshan. He was given the name Chos-rgyal-lhun-po
at birth. He received his rab-byung vows from the Mkhan-
chen Kun-dga'-sangs-rgyas, who bestowed upon him the name
